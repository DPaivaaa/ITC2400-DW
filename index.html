MEET OUR HOSTS 

Welcome to our cozy corner! Our passionate hosts are here to make your visit purr-fect, serving up delightful brews and warm smiles in the company of our adorable feline friends. Each of us is dedicated to creating a relaxing, joyful experience for you, whether you’re here for the coffee, the cats, or a little bit of both. We can’t wait to make you feel right at home!

Whether you’re here to enjoy a quiet moment with a latte or to make new feline friends, our hosts are on hand to guide you through the experience. Each visit supports local animal welfare efforts, and some of our kitties may even be available for adoption. Feel free to ask our hosts any questions about our cats, the coffee, or how you can get involved in our mission. We look forward to making your day a little brighter!

Felix

Meet Felix, our curious and playful tabby with a heart of gold! Always the first to greet new guests, Felix loves a good chin scratch and a game of chase with feather toys. He’s a charming little character who can usually be found lounging in the sunniest spots or pouncing on his favorite ball. Felix will happily settle into your lap, adding a bit of warmth and joy to your coffee break.

Nala

Nala is our gentle queen, with striking green eyes and a coat as soft as silk. She has a quiet, graceful presence and enjoys watching the world go by from her favorite perch. While she may seem shy at first, Nala loves to cuddle once she feels comfortable, purring contentedly as you sip your coffee. Her calm demeanor is perfect for those looking for a peaceful moment with a feline friend.

Aurora

Our resident bundle of energy, Aurora, is always ready to play! This friendly calico has a knack for making everyone smile, whether she’s chasing her tail or "helping" our baristas behind the counter. Aurora loves interacting with guests and is quick to make friends, so don’t be surprised if she’s the first to sit by your side and keep you company.

Midnight

Mystery and charm meet in Midnight, our sleek black cat with a soft spot for gentle pets. Midnight might seem a bit aloof at first, but give him a little time, and he’ll warm up with a loving nuzzle or two. He’s a master of finding cozy corners and curling up for a nap, creating the perfect relaxed vibe for any visitor who needs a quiet companion.
